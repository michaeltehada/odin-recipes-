Margarita

Description

Mikes margarita is created with the perfect ratio of ingredients that will give you the best-tasting margarita every single time.

Ingredients

  • Tequila
  • Triple sec
  • Lime
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingrigents
  2. Moisten the rim of a glass with a lime wedge. Sprinkle salt onto a plate. Lightly dip the moistened rim into the salt.
  3. Place a large ice cube in the glass and freeze the prepared glass until ready to serve.
  4. Fill a cocktail shaker with fresh ice. Add tequila, triple sec, and lime juice. Cover and shake vigorously until the outside of the shaker has frosted.
  5. Strain margarita into the chilled glass and garnish with a slice of lime.
